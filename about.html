About Us

Here at Desi bites our goal is to serve fresh, homemade, and healthy Pakistani foods. We accomplish this by handpicking the ingredients that are used in all of our dishes. Each ingredient goes through a strict process to see if its safe to eat and what this tactic does is that it enhances the flavor of each one of our dishes. All of our spices are processed and made in-house to ensure the freshness in each of our dishes. This combination leads to each one of our dishes to have high nutritive value, yet are still are mouth wateringly delicious meals.
